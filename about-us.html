HISTORY OF THE MERCER CLUB
The Mercer Club is a New York city based subscription service founded by Chike Achebe and Travis Ezidiegwu in 2019 with the intent to grow sustainable fashion through our online community. Our mission statement is rooted in the belief that the fashion industryâ€™s global carbon footprint has ballooned out of control and that something must be done to the slow danger it presents to worldwide climate change. With that in mind (and a lifelong love of avant garde fashion) the Mercer Club was conceived. We aim to not only bring sustainable fashion to more people but also recreate the digital community of artists, musicians and creators we grew up being so inspired by.
